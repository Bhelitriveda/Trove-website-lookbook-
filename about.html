Our Story

The Journey
behind trove

Jaya & Mansi

Made with hands.
Carried with heart.

Hello, we're Jaya and Mansi, the creators behind Trove. Our journey didn't begin with a business plan or a grand vision. It began with a shared love for art, creativity, and the desire to create something meaningful with our own hands.

Like many creative people, we spent years exploring different forms of design and artistic expression. We were fascinated by how a simple piece of art could transform a space, evoke emotions, and tell a story without saying a single word. That fascination eventually became the foundation of Trove.

The name Trove represents a collection of treasures, and that's exactly what we wanted our creations to be — special pieces that people could cherish and connect with.

What started as experimenting with paints, fabrics, and ideas soon turned into countless hours of learning, practicing, making mistakes, and improving. We painted late into the night, tested new techniques, redesigned products, and slowly built confidence in our craft.

The journey wasn't always easy. Building a handmade brand comes with challenges. Every product requires time, patience, and attention to detail. There were moments of uncertainty, moments when growth felt slow, and moments when we questioned whether people would truly value handcrafted art in a world dominated by mass production.

But each message from a customer, every order, and every kind word reminded us why we started. Today, Trove is more than a brand. It is a reflection of our artistic journey, our values, and our belief that handmade creations have a special place in people's lives.

Every tapestry, bag, and hand-painted piece we create carries a part of our story. Behind each design are hours of sketching, painting, refining, and dreaming. We believe that art should feel personal, and that's why we approach every creation with care and intention.

As we continue to grow, our mission remains simple: to create meaningful art that inspires, connects, and brings beauty into everyday life. To everyone who has supported us — thank you for being part of this journey. This is only the beginning.

— Jaya & Mansi
